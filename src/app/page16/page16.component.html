#14: Stand Feet Shoulder Width Apart on Foam, Eyes Closed

Tester: Place Tempur® foam on floor. Assist client stepping onto and then off the foam after testing. Allow 2 trials, if needed. Have client step off the foam between trials. Time the task. Score best trial.

Client: Step on the foam, place your feet shoulder width apart, hands on your hips. I will ask you to close your eyes and stand as still as you can until I say stop. I will start timing when you close your eyes.
